# Spider-Man (2002)

## Overview
*Spider-Man* is a 2002 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. Directed by Sam Raimi, it was the first installment in the *Spider-Man* film series and stars Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker / Spider-Man. The film tells the origin story of Spider-Man, from Peter's transformation after a radioactive spider bite to his fight against the villain Norman Osborn (Willem Dafoe), who becomes the Green Goblin.

## Plot Summary
Peter Parker, a high school student in New York City, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a genetically altered spider. After the death of his Uncle Ben, Peter embraces his new powers and becomes the masked hero Spider-Man. Meanwhile, a scientist named Norman Osborn develops an experimental formula that transforms him into the villainous Green Goblin. As Spider-Man faces the Green Goblin, Peter must also balance his personal life with his responsibilities as a superhero.
